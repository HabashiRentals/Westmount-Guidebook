food and culture guide ABOUT THE AREA
07

Nestled along the shores of Lake Simcoe, Innisfil's Alcona community offers the perfect mix of small-town warmth and big-value convenience. Just 5 minutes from a beautiful sandy beach and within easy reach of Friday Harbour, Tanger Outlets, and some genuinely great local food — this is a neighbourhood that surprises people. Whether you're here to relax, explore, or eat your way through the area, you'll leave with a list of reasons to come back.

explore beyond DAY TRIPS
08

Within an hour's drive you have some of Ontario's best destinations. Use your stay as a base.

~40 min · Beach Town
Wasaga Beach
The world's longest freshwater beach. Swim, sunbathe, explore scenic trails, and grab food along the strip. A classic Ontario summer day out.
~45 min · City
Downtown Barrie
Waterfront promenade, Flying Monkeys Craft Brewery, great restaurants, and a vibrant weekend market. Easy half-day trip up the 400.
~50 min · Mountain Resort
Blue Mountain
Year-round destination with skiing in winter, hiking and biking in summer, and a vibrant village with restaurants and shops. An Ontario classic.
~55 min South · City
Downtown Toronto
CN Tower, St. Lawrence Market, the Distillery District, world-class dining. The full city experience is just under an hour away on the 400.
~45 min · Ski Resort
Mount St. Louis Moonstone
Top Ontario ski destination in winter. Day passes, equipment rentals, and lessons available for all skill levels. Book early for weekends.
~55 min · Outlet Shopping
Premium Outlets Toronto
Hundreds of designer and brand-name stores at outlet prices. A natural extension of the Tanger trip if you're serious about shopping.
